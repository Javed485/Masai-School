You are given a positive number, stored in a variable with the name num
You need to find the factorial of the number.
In simple words, the factorial of a number is the product of all positive integers less than or equal to a
given positive integer.
For example, you are given a number num = 5.
According to the definition, the factorial ofnumwill be5*4*3*2*1 = 120, which is the required answer.
Note: Factorial of 0 is 1.
